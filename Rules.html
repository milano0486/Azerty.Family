Server Rules Azerty Family

  1. Be Respectful
    Treat everyone with kindness and respect. No rude, mean, or harassing comments.
  2. No Inappropriate Language
    Keep profanity to a minimum. No hate speech or slurs. Swearing is allowed only in casual channels — not in help, support, or code-related channels.
  3. No Spamming
    Avoid sending multiple short messages in a row. Use full sentences (5+ words) and keep chats readable.
  4. No NSFW Content
    This server is a safe space for creativity. No pornographic, adult, or other NSFW content allowed.
  5. No Advertisements
    Unsolicited promotion of servers, products, or streams is not allowed. Share relevant content only in approved media channels.
  6. No Offensive Names or Profile Pictures
    Keep your display name and profile picture appropriate.
  7. No Raiding
    Server raiding is against Discord’s Terms of Service and will lead to a permanent ban.
  8. No Threats
    Threats of any kind are strictly forbidden.
  9. Follow Discord Community Guidelines
    Make sure to always follow Discord’s official guidelines and Azerty Family rules.
  10. Ask Before Joining Voice Channels
    Do not join active voice channels without asking. If others say no, respect their space.
