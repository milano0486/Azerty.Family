Frequently Asked Questions

What is Azerty Family?

Azerty Family is a chill and inclusive online community where members can game, chat, watch movies, and hang out in a relaxed, respectful environment.

What types of games do you play?

We play a wide variety of games based on what the community enjoys — from competitive and co-op games to casual and party titles. Everyone is welcome, regardless of skill level.

3. Who can join the community?

Anyone with a positive attitude and a respectful approach is welcome to join. We’re open to gamers, movie lovers, or anyone looking to relax and connect with others.

4. Do you host events or game nights?

Yes! We occasionally host game nights, movie watch parties, and community hangouts. Keep an eye on announcements and event channels for updates.

5. How can I contribute or get more involved?

Simply being active and respectful is a great start. You can also suggest activities, host your own sessions, or reach out to moderators if you’d like to help with community projects.

6. What if I need help or have an issue?

If you have questions or need support, feel free to reach out to a moderator or post in the designated help or general chat channels. We're here to help.
